Dragon's Grill

The snappy food of asian fusion

Snappy food is tastier than fast food and
more convenient than sit-down.

Dragon's Grill will be:
Fresh
Delicious
Healthy
Fast

Menu

1) Choice of meat
Chicken, Steak, or Pork

2) Choice of style
Korean bulgogi, Japanese teriyaki,
Thai honey BBQ, or Vietnamese marinade

3) Choice of bowl
Regular or large portion of steamed rice

Bowls garnished with steamed carrots and broccoli
splashed with a clear tentsuyu sesame sauce.

Sides:
Wonton soup (pork or vegetable)
Warm salted edamame
Spinach and cucumber salad
Steamed carrots and broccoli

Drinks:
Meals include self-serve green tea and barley tea

Desserts:
Mochi ball ice cream
Pineapple shortcake
Egg puff waffle with black sesame sauce
