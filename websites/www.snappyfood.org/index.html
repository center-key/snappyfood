Snappy Food

Tastier than fast food, more convenient than sit-down

Snappy food (a.k.a. "fast casual") is not fast food.

Snappy food is:
Fresh
Delicious
Healthy
Fast

Customers at restaurants serving snappy food place their orders at the counter and then enjoy the convenience of a quickly prepared meal.

The ambiance is clean and modern and the food is prepared with fresh ingredients — grilled or steamed, never fried.

The Dragon's Grill restaurant will be the snappy food purveyor of asian fusion.
